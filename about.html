About Us

Our Company History

IELTS Writing AI was founded in 2019 with a vision to revolutionize the way individuals prepare for the IELTS writing test. It started as a small team of language experts and technology enthusiasts who recognized the challenges faced by test takers in improving their writing skills.

Since its inception, IELTS Writing AI has been dedicated to developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence algorithms that provide personalized feedback on grammar, vocabulary, and sentence structure. Through continuous research and innovation, we have refined our platform to deliver accurate assessments and valuable insights to users.

Over the years, we have proudly helped thousands of test takers improve their writing abilities and achieve their desired band scores. Our commitment to excellence, user-centric approach, and focus on continuous improvement have made IELTS Writing AI a trusted companion for test preparation.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals to succeed in the IELTS writing test by providing them with an intelligent and user-friendly platform that enhances their writing skills. We aim to make the process of preparing for the IELTS writing test efficient, effective, and accessible to learners of all levels.

Our Team

Our team consists of passionate language experts, skilled developers, and dedicated support professionals. Together, we strive to create an outstanding user experience and deliver the highest quality of service to our users.
